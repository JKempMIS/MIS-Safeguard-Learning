Introduction and Welcome

Dear MIS Staff,

Welcome to the MIS Safeguard training module. The safety and well-being of our students is our top priority. This module provides essential information on our school's child protection and safeguarding policies and your role in upholding them. Your active participation in this training is crucial in creating and maintaining a safe and supportive learning environment for all our students.

Overview of the Importance of Child Protection and Safeguarding

Mooltripakdee International School is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all students. This module will cover the policies and procedures we have in place to protect children from harm, including maltreatment, abuse, and bullying. It is the responsibility of every staff member to be aware of these policies and to act in accordance with them.

Instructions on How to Navigate the Module

  1. Introduction and Welcome

  3. Glossary of Key Terms
  4. Reflection Activity

Please complete each section in order. The quiz will assess your understanding of the material, and the reflection activity will help you apply what you've learnt to your daily practice.

Glossary of Key Terms

The following terms are defined as they are used in the MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy and the MIS Anti-Bullying Policy:

Term Definition Relevant Policy Section
Abuse The maltreatment of a child in any form. MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy, Section 6
Bullying Persistent behaviour by an individual or group with the intention of verbally, physically, or emotionally harming another person or group. MIS Anti-Bullying Policy, Section 2
Child Protection The protection of children from violence, exploitation, abuse, and neglect. MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy, Section 2
Designated Safeguarding Lead (DSL) The head of our school's Safeguarding Team. MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy, Section 2
Guardian/s Any individual involved in the care of a child outside of school hours. MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy, Section 2
Harm Impairment of the health, safety or development of a child. MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy, Section 1
Neglect The failure to provide for a childâ€™s basic needs. MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy, Section 6
Safeguarding The action that is taken to promote the welfare of children and protect them from harm. MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy, Section 2
Senior Management Team Head of School, Deputy Head of School, Heads of Sections (Early Years, Primary and Secondary), Head of Personnel and School Coordinator. MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy, Section 2
Vulnerable Pupils Pupils who are more likely to be targets of bullying. MIS Anti-Bullying Policy, Section 2
Welfare The physical, emotional, mental, and general well-being of a child, both at school and in other environments. MIS Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy, Section 2
